## Our Story

      

### 🚣‍♂️ **A Journey Born from Passion and Necessity**

 

Rowing with Watts began in 2023, not just as a website, but as a mission fueled by personal experience and a profound love for indoor rowing. As a CrossFit enthusiast sidelined by a knee injury, I discovered the world of indoor rowing. It was a revelation — a perfect blend of low-impact exercise and intense physical challenge. 

 

However, I quickly realized there was a significant gap in the online space for in-depth, research-backed information about the effects of indoor rowing on the body. This lack of comprehensive resources sparked an idea.

 

### 💡 **Filling the Void with Evidence and Expertise**

 

This is how Rowing with Watts was born — out of a desire to provide a platform where fitness and scientific research intersect, offering insights into the transformative power of rowing. Our goal is to go beyond just reviewing products; we aim to enlighten, educate, and inspire. 

 

We are here to share everything from the technical nitty-gritty to personal rowing challenges, all backed by professional expertise and a commitment to unbiased, evidence-based content. Join us in this quest to explore the depths of indoor rowing and be part of a community that values health, knowledge, and the joy of rowing.

## Our Responsibility

 

### Upholding Editorial Integrity

 

At Rowing with Watts, we are deeply committed to editorial integrity. Our approach is built on:

 
 
- **Unbiased Information**: Ensuring our reviews and guides are free from brand influence. 
- **Meticulous Research**: Each article is a result of thorough research, reflecting our commitment to accuracy. 
- **Informed Decision Making**: Empowering our readers with facts, enabling them to make well-informed choices. 
 

### Commitment to Evidence-Based Content

 

Our content is the cornerstone of our credibility, characterized by:

 
 
- **Scientific Accuracy**: Drawing from peer-reviewed papers and expert opinions. 
- **Verifiable Data**: Ensuring all information is current and scientifically validated. 
- **Expert Review**: Each piece of content is scrutinized by professionals for accuracy and relevance. 
 

[Learn about how we select products featured on this site](https://rowingwithwatts.com/review-guidelines/)

 

### Ethical and Transparent Affiliate Marketing

 

While we engage in affiliate marketing, our principles guide our monetization strategy:

 
 
- **Merit-Based Recommendations**: Products are recommended based on quality and suitability, not commission potential. 
- **Transparency with Readers**: Open communication about the use of affiliate links. 
- **Preserving Trust**: Ensuring our financial incentives do not compromise content objectivity. 
 

### Championing Sustainability

 
 
- **Eco-Friendly Brands**: Prioritizing partnerships with brands committed to using recyclable materials and sustainable practices. 
- **Environmental Impact**: Actively promoting products that contribute to a lower environmental footprint. 
- **Reader Engagement**: Encouraging our audience to make eco-conscious choices and participate in sustainable initiatives.
