## How We Review Products

 

At Rowing With Watts, we're committed to providing our readers with the most accurate, reliable, and helpful reviews on indoor rowing machines, accessories, and apps. 

 

We are committed to maintaining the utmost standards in our product recommendations. We believe it's important for you to understand the meticulous process behind how we select the products featured in our guides.

 

Here's how we ensure our reviews are trustworthy and valuable:

 

## We do:

 

_Here is everything we look for before we feature a rowing machine, its accessories or rowing machine apps on our pages._

 

## Look for regulatory authorities’ approval

 

While indoor rowing equipment and apps aren't directly regulated by specific governing bodies, at Rowing With Watts, we proactively monitor guidelines and statements from authoritative rowing organizations. This vigilance ensures that our readers are well-informed and protected from any potential issues highlighted by these authorities:

 
 
- **[World Rowing (FISA)](https://worldrowing.com/)** 
- **[US Rowing](https://usrowing.org/)** 
- **[British Rowing](https://www.britishrowing.org/)** 
- **[U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC)](https://www.cpsc.gov/)** 
 

Our goal is to stay ahead of the curve, ensuring the safety and satisfaction of our rowing community.

 

## Thoroughly examine user review feedback

 

Brands could easily handpick their best products and send them to us for glowing reviews.

 

However, at Rowing With Watts, we prioritize genuine consumer insights to form our opinions on product quality.

 

We thoroughly investigate both favorable and critical reviews of all the products we recommend.

 

Frequently, we connect directly with customers to gain a deeper understanding of their experiences, ensuring our content accurately reflects real-world usage.

### Review brand history and product range

 

Have you ever heard the saying "History repeats itself"? We think this applies to indoor rowing products too.

 

That's why, before we recommend a new product or brand at Rowing With Watts, we look deeply into their history and operations to evaluate potential risks for you.

 

Here are some key aspects we consider when evaluating a new product or brand:

 
 
- Historical recalls of the brand and product line. 
- Recall history of the manufacturing facilities. 
- Any warnings or recalls issued by regulatory bodies. 
- Location of the product's manufacture. 
- Affiliations of the brand. 
- Changes in brand name that might indicate an attempt to conceal past issues. 
- Recent changes in ownership, manufacturing processes, or product designs. 
 

## Analyze Performance Data & Consult Rowing Experts

 

Before featuring a product on Rowing With Watts, our team of rowing professionals and fitness experts meticulously reviews the performance data provided by the manufacturer, as well as independent testing results.

 

This rigorous process ensures that every product in our buying guides meets high standards for effectiveness and contributes positively to your indoor rowing experience.

 

## Tailoring to Specific Rowing Needs

 

Since our guides often address specific needs, such as beginners or advanced rowers, we ensure our expert team confirms that the product recommendations are optimal for the intended audience.

 

## Assessing Marketing Claims

 

Rowing equipment brands frequently make bold claims about their products. 

 

We meticulously analyze their marketing materials to provide you with clear, factual information about what you're investing in.

  

## Understanding Pricing and Product Positioning

  

Rowing equipment comes at various price points and features. Our guides include a diverse range of options, ensuring there's something for every budget – from economical to premium choices. 

 

We also consider special requirements like advanced performance features or compact designs.

 

## Exploring Delivery and Accessibility

 

We understand the importance of receiving your rowing equipment promptly and without hassle. Therefore, we evaluate average delivery times, costs, geographical coverage, and return policies. 

 

Brands offering better terms receive higher recommendations in our guides.

 

## Incorporating Your Feedback

 

At the end of each article, we include a rating widget for you to share your thoughts. 

 

Your feedback is crucial in shaping our content and ensuring it meets your needs and expectations.

 

## We Don't:

 

_Things you will NEVER find on Rowing With Watts._

 

## Take sponsorship fees

 

We regularly receive offers from brands seeking prominent placement on our site. However, we consistently decline these offers. 

 

Our commitment is to our readers and the integrity of our content, not financial incentives. Our focus is on delivering genuine, unbiased recommendations for indoor rowing products.

 

## Publish content without comprehensive research

 

Every article on Rowing With Watts undergoes an extensive research process. We don't make recommendations lightly. 

 

Rest assured, if you read it on our site, it has been thoroughly vetted through our stringent criteria. You can trust the advice we provide, knowing it's backed by thorough research and expertise.

 

## Go against Expert and Regulatory Guidelines

 

In a world where challenging the status quo is often celebrated, we maintain a respectful stance towards professional advice and regulatory guidelines in the rowing industry. 

 

If an expert or regulatory body advises against a certain practice or product, we take that seriously. 

 

Our content reflects this respect for professional guidance, ensuring that we provide safe, reliable, and trustworthy information.
